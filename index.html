Hey, I'm Sonya


I'M A NYC BASED FULL STACK DEVELOPER

I took my first programming course my sophomore year of high school. It was a web development class, and ever since then I've been hooked. I loved wielding the ability to make things magically appear on the screen with just the tips of my fingers. I became obsessed with beautiful typography, crisp color combinations, and aesthetically pleasing graphics. I didn't know this back then, but this would serve as my gateway to the world of Computer Science.

Several years later & I am still programming, learning more than ever. Since then, I have picked up skills across the entire web stack, from front-end development to server-side and back-end languages and frameworks. I've previously worked at AlleyNYC, where I designed the UI/UX of their website, and at Liquidnet, where I created internal tools using Java/Spring & AngularJS.
